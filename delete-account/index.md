# Delete your ASA BBQ account

**Last updated: 14 May 2026**

This page explains how to delete your ASA BBQ customer-app account and the order history tied to it. You don't need to sign in to read this page.

The ASA BBQ customer app is distributed on Google Play under the package name `com.asabbq.customer_mobile`. It is operated by the ASA BBQ restaurant team in Bogo City, Cebu, Philippines.

---

## The shortest version

Email **support@asabbq.com** from the email address you used to sign in to the app, with the subject line **"Delete my account"**. Within **7 days** we will confirm we've removed your account and the orders attached to it.

If your last order is still being prepared or paid for, we'll wait for that order to close before deleting the account, so the kitchen and the cashier don't get a half-finished order with no customer attached.

---

## Step-by-step

1. Open your email and write a message to **support@asabbq.com**.
2. Send it from the **same Google account** you used to sign in to the app — that's how we confirm the request is really from you.
3. Use the subject line **"Delete my account"**.
4. (Optional) If you remember an order number or a date of a recent order, mention it. It helps us find the account quickly. You do not have to include this.
5. Send.

We will reply, confirm, and then run the deletion. You'll receive a final confirmation email when it's done.

---

## What gets deleted

When the deletion completes, the following are removed from our servers:

- Your account in our authentication system (the link between your Google sign-in and our user record).
- The orders you placed through the app, including the items, totals, notes, and GCash reference numbers attached to them.
- Any saved profile fields (the name shown on your orders, the picture link from your Google profile).

After deletion finishes, signing in with the same Google account creates a fresh account — the previous one is gone, not deactivated.

## What we retain, and for how long

Some things are retained for a short period for legal and accounting reasons:

- **Bookkeeping records of past sales.** Philippine tax law (BIR) requires us to keep records of every Sales Invoice we issue for several years. Where an order of yours was finalised into a Sales Invoice, the **sale itself** stays in our records as required by law — but it is decoupled from your account. Once your account is deleted, those sale records are no longer linked to any identifiable customer.
- **Authentication and security logs** kept by our backend host (Supabase) — for example, sign-in timestamps and IP addresses tied to the deleted account ID — may persist briefly in their infrastructure logs for fraud-prevention and platform security purposes. These are not visible to us as user records.

We do **not** retain a personally identifiable copy of your order history once deletion is complete.

---

## Removing data from your phone

The deletion flow above clears the data on our servers. To clear data still cached on the device:

- **Sign out** from inside the app to flush the cached session.
- **Uninstall** the ASA BBQ app from Android Settings → Apps → ASA BBQ → Uninstall. Uninstalling removes the cart, theme preference, and any cached menu images.

Uninstalling alone does **not** delete your server-side account — email us as described above if you want both gone.

---

## Can't sign in or no longer have access to the Google account?

If you've lost access to the Google account you used to sign in, you cannot prove the account is yours just by emailing us from a different address. In that case, write to **support@asabbq.com** with as much detail as you can about your past orders — approximate dates, items ordered, the table you sat at, the GCash reference number on a past payment — and we'll do our best to identify the account and delete it.
